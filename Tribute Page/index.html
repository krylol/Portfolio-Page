Raymond Reddington

Dedicated to the life of Red

Raymond Reddington attended the United States Naval Academy, graduating at the top of his class at the age of 24, and was being groomed for admiral when he disappeared while coming home to visit his family for Christmas in 1990.

Four years later he resurfaced, selling classified documents to the enemy, and was charged with treason in absentia. Over the next 20 years he built a syndicate of contacts: spies, thieves, smugglers, drug traffickers, people smugglers, human traffickers, arms traffickers, counterfeiters, forgers, hackers, mercenaries, and assassins. During this time, U.S. assets located in Moscow, Islamabad, and Beijing were compromised.

He infiltrated the private sector, and there was no industry that was out of his reach, including technology, shipping, communications, security/military contracting and pharmaceuticals. Eventually he went from just selling secrets to starting wars, toppling governments, and influencing geopolitics to suit his needs. He became known as “the Concierge of Crime” due to his ability to arrange deals in the black market.

He was ranked fourth, and later first, on the FBI Ten Most Wanted Fugitives list as an “arrest on sight” fugitive, as shown when he surrendered to the FBI in “Pilot”. In a flashback in “Requiem”, Red reveals to Kathryn Nemec that he created his criminal network with the sole purpose of protecting Elizabeth Keen and when his enemies got too close, went to the FBI to ensure her protection more directly.

    Raymond Skills

  • He is an excellent communicator. He has an uncanny ability to talk himself out of any problem. He has excellent social skills and is always willing to share a story. Everything seems natural and genuine as if he were a word magician who could communicate with anyone at any moment.
  • He always has a backup plan. He is always willing to improvise and alter his original strategy if necessary. If things go wrong, he reacts quickly. He expects everything to go as planned, but he constantly prepares for the worst.
  • He treats everyone with respect. It is obvious that he has an excellent education and is kind and respectful to everyone around him. It's a tremendous pleasure to accompany him, even though he's the evil guy.
  • He has a keen ability to read people. His most significant quality is his ability to read people. He is meticulous in his attention to detail and can discern the moods and emotions of others. Almost every time, he knows what the others are up to.
  • He is a master planner. When it comes to planning and scheming, he excels. He understands how to work with his team and how to consider all of the necessary aspects to complete tasks correctly. He gives the appearance that he is unfazed by anything.
